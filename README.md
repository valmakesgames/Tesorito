# Tesoro

A story about leaving the stars to return to Earth.

Recollect memories from the stars to bring you to your once human form again. Someone down there needs you.

> "Come back to Earth, Star Child."

## Equip Menu Prototype
On your journey from the stars and down back to Earth, you will unlock some of your old memories. One by one, pieces of your humanity will be restored.

![Equip Menu mockup to help define the story for the Equip Menu prototype.](https://github.com/valmakesgames/Tesorito/blob/master/art_tesorito/equip-menu_mockup_v1.png)

## Functionality
- Player can equip one item from each category of equipment: Shoes, Pants, Shirts, Gloves, and Hats.
- Each piece of equipment has both a weight value (0 - 999) and a defense value (0 - 9999)
- Each piece of equipment has 0 - 3 Perks
- Only one Legendary Item may be equipped at any time.
- When a player surpasses 70lbs total weight from equipment load, an error is shown
